In-Tension Survey

Building and managing websites for the College of Engineering and its departments involves balancing the needs and priorities of a diverse set of stakeholders. If you've received this survey, you're one of them.

We'd like to thank you for your participation in our redesign process so far and ask one more favor to help guide our decisions.

This survey plots competing interests on a continuum. For each set of options, tick the score that best represents what YOU consider the IDEAL BALANCE for YOUR website.

Consider zero a fulcrum, and avoid choosing it unless you truly have no preference one way or the other. In each case we intend to design for both interests, but not to the same degree.
