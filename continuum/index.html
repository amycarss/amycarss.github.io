In-Tension Survey

Building and managing websites for the College of Engineering and its departments involves balancing the needs and priorities of a diverse set of stakeholders. If you've received this survey, you're one of them.

We'd like to thank you for your participation in our redesign process so far and ask one more favor to help us prioritize components in our design system.

This survey plots competing interests on a continuum. For each set of options, tick the score that best represents YOUR PRIORITY IN AN IDEAL WORLD with regard to your website. Keep in mind we are not trying to create consensus, but rather trying to understand the strength of the pull in each direction.
