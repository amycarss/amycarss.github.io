Candidate Evaluation Tool

The ADVANCE office offers a method for departmental faculty to provide evaluations of faculty candidates in an unbiased and confidential manner.  This method consists of a web form that can be customized to meet specific departmental needs.  For further information please contact ADVANCE at advanceprogram@umich.edu.
