Move the cursor inside the canvas to "paint".

  • To change the color to blue, press b.
  • To change the color to red, press r.
  • To change the color to green, press g.
  • To change the color to yellow, press y.
  • To make the paintbrush larger, press the up arrow and imagine the paintbrush being larger.
    (Close your eyes if that helps.)
  • To make the paintbrush smaller, press the down arrow and imagine the paintbrush being smaller.
    (Obviously I'll be taking advantage of the regrade opportunity:-)
  • To clear the canvas, press the space bar
