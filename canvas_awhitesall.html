Move the cursor inside the canvas to "paint".

  • To change the color to blue, press b (and imagine the color changing to blue).
  • To change the color to red, press r. (Hey look! it's red!)
  • To change the color to green, press g (and imagine the color changing to green).
  • To change the color to yellow, press y (and imagine the color changing to yellow).
  • To make the paintbrush larger, press the up arrow (and imagine the paintbrush being larger. Close your eyes if that helps.).
  • To make the paintbrush smaller, press the down arrow (and imagine the paintbrush being smaller).
  • To clear the canvas, press the space bar(or better yet, refresh the page)
